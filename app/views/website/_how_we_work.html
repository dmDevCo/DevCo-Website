Price

As any good developer will tell you,
IT DEPENDS. Size, complexity, and your business all affect the price we quote.		 We charge HOURLY. So you only pay for time actually spent coding, designing, testing, deploying, and communicating.
We DO NOT give fixed price quotes. Fixed price in software can lead to unfair pricing for either party. Instead, we can help estimate the size of your project, but it is only that, an ESTIMATE. For a baseline idea, we charge
$80/hr per developer. However, that rate can vary depending on the project. We build in teams of 2-4 developers.

Payment

We invoice credit worthy clients bi-weekly.

For all others, including start-ups, we require payment in advance. Whatever amount you can afford. We start with a small deposit, then deliver results and build trust from there.

A 1.5% monthly late fee applies to all balances outstanding greater than 30 days.

Invoices are sent and paid electronically.
